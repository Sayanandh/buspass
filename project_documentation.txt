## For Students

### 1. Getting Started
- Visit our sleek, modern website
- Look for the prominent "Register" or "Login" buttons
- Choose your preferred option

### 2. Registration Process
1. Click the "Register" button (highlighted in blue)
2. Fill in your details in our clean, organized form:
   - Your full name
   - College ID number
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Create a secure password
3. Click the "Submit" button
4. Check your email for verification

### 3. Logging In
1. Enter your credentials in the modern login form
2. Click the "Login" button
3. Use the "Forgot Password" link if needed

### 4. Booking a Bus Pass
1. Click the prominent "Book New Pass" button
2. Use our interactive journey selector:
   - Pick your location from the dropdown
   - Choose your destination
   - Select date from the calendar
   - Pick time from the time selector
3. Use our visual seat selector:
   - Interactive bus layout
   - Color-coded seats (Green: Available, Red: Occupied)
   - Hover effects for better visibility
4. Review your booking in the summary card
5. Complete payment in our secure payment portal

### 5. Managing Your Passes
- View passes in our card-based layout
- Download tickets with one click
- Check bus status in real-time
- Cancel passes through our simple interface

### 6. Tracking Your Bus
- Interactive map showing bus location
- Real-time updates with smooth animations
- Clear estimated arrival time display
- Push notifications for important updates
